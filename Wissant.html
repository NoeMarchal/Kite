Wissant

Wissant is a beautiful place with a beautiful
countryside with miles of beach at your disposal.

Remark: You can ride during the high tide but
sometime you need to land/launch your kite
in the dunes so it's a little dangerous

difficulty: HARD
From beginner to confirmed but be careful
it is far from being the easiest spot to ride!!

Wind:
One of the windiest spot in the north!
Ideal all year round, but less windy in summer.

Wave:
From 20 knots there is possibility of big waves when the wind is going South-West.
Also when it's high tide and the wind is South/South West,
not too strong there are some beautiful/ sometimes big waves.
BE CAREFUL!! ONLY for surfing
because the wind is offshore
