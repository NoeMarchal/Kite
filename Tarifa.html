Tarifa

Tarifa is a beautiful place with a beautiful countryside.
Nearly always sunny and windy, this is one of the best spot in the world.

difficulty: HARD
From advanced to confirmed,
you need to have the capacity to ride upwind

Wind:
One of the windiest spot in Spain during April until October.
The wind is nearly always offshore or onshore.

Wave:
There are rarely big waves in the afternoon but in the morning yes.
Check the windguru forecast to know what to expect.
